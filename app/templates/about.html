About Us

IOI Vismaya

IOI Vismaya is an educational platform developed for grade 9-12 students at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

Our primary goal is to provide a safe, focused environment where students can practice algorithmic thinking, data structures, and mathematics to prepare for the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI).

This platform facilitates weekly challenges, a live leaderboard, and performance tracking strictly for educational purposes. We are dedicated to fostering problem-solving skills in high school students across the country.
