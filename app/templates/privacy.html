Privacy Policy

Data Collection

We only collect basic information necessary for student authentication and verification. This includes your name, email address, phone number, school, and grade. We do not collect financial or sensitive personal information.

Data Usage

The collected data is used exclusively to:

  • Verify your student status.
  • Maintain your weekly challenge streak and leaderboard rankings.
  • Send One-Time Passwords (OTPs) to secure your account.

Data Protection

Passwords are securely hashed using modern cryptographic standards (bcrypt). We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute user data to any third parties. All information is kept strictly confidential within the context of the IOI Vismaya educational program.
